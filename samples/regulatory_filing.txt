Item 2. Management's Discussion and Analysis

OVERVIEW
CyberTech Innovations Inc. provides advanced cybersecurity solutions to enterprise clients. Our revenue decreased 8% compared to the same period last year due to increased competition and delayed customer implementations.

RISK FACTORS
We face various risk factors including:
- Cybersecurity threats to our own systems
- Regulatory changes affecting our industry
- Competition from larger technology companies
- Customer concentration in government contracts

LIQUIDITY AND CAPITAL RESOURCES
Our primary sources of liquidity are cash flows from operations and available credit facilities. We believe our current liquidity position is adequate to meet operational needs for the next twelve months.

Item 3. Quantitative and Qualitative Disclosures About Market Risk

We are exposed to market risks including interest rate risk on our variable-rate debt and foreign currency exchange risk from international operations.
